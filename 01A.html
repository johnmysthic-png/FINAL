Our Story

AMT Engineering Works isn't just a shop; it’s a testament to hard work and perseverance. Built from the ground up by the Lapuz Family, our company started as a small dream fueled by grit and a deep understanding of metalwork.

"Put in the work when others won't, so you can find peace when others can't."

Being family-owned means we don't answer to corporate boards; we answer to you. We treat every blueprint that comes through our doors with the same care we gave our very first project. Our growth is rooted in the trust of our local partners and the specialized skills passed down through the family.
